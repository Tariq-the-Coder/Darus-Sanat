Good News for the Students

A good news for the people of Qadian and its suburbs and all residents of other states that under the auspices of jamaat Ahmadiyya Qadian a Technical Training Centre has been established in affiliation with NSIC, Govt. of India where Vocational (Technical) training is imparted to yougsters having passed 8th and 10th at a very competitive fee structure so that the skilled youth may avail the Job opportunities within India or Abroad.
